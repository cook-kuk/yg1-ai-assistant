# i-Xmill — XB1N
**제품명**: i-Xmill BALL INSERTS / XB1N SERIES

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Ball Insert / Indexable Ball End Mill
- 코팅: extremely abrasive resistant coating
- 직경 범위: D8.0~D32.0

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K

## 대상 피삭재
- Non-alloy steel
- Low alloy steel
- High alloyed steel
- Tool steel
- Grey cast iron
- Nodular cast iron
- Malleable cast iron

## 특징
- Indexable Ball End Mill for economic use
- Three types of inserts are available
- For General Purpose (~HRC50)
- Special geometry
- Excellent performance
- General purpose ball inserts
- Recommended cutting conditions provided by ISO material group and tool diameter

## 용도
- General purpose machining

## 선정 가이드
- ◎ : Excellent, ○ : Good. XB1N is recommended for general purpose materials in ISO P/M/K, with strong suitability across steels and cast irons shown in the matrix.
