# YG-1 — STI
**제품명**: HSS-E & HSS SCREW THREAD INSERT TAPS / Tapping STI Threads of Soft Materials

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Screw Thread Insert Tap
- 공구 재질: HSS-E / HSS
- 코팅: Bright
- 헬릭스각: R40
- 깊이비: Max. 2.5xD / Max. 2.0xD

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- P: Non-alloy steel
- P: Low alloy steel
- P: High alloyed steel and tool steel
- M: Stainless steel
- K: Grey cast iron
- K: Nodular cast iron
- K: Malleable cast iron
- N: Aluminum wrought alloy
- N: Aluminum cast alloy
- N: Copper and Copper Alloys (Bronze / Brass)
- N: Non Metallic Materials
- S: Heat Resistant Super Alloys
- S: Titanium Alloys
- H: Hardened steel
- H: Chilled Cast Iron
- H: Hardened Cast Iron

## 특징
- 선정용 가이드로 STI 나사 가공용 탭의 재질/홀 타입별 적용을 안내함
- Max. 2.5xD Blind Hole에는 HSS-E, Spiral Flute, 2.5P chamfer lead 적용
- Max. 2.0xD Blind/Through Hole에는 HSS, Straight Flute, 1.5P/5.0P chamfer lead 적용
- 표면처리 Bright
- M/MF, UNC/F, W, M-LH / W-LH, PIPE TAPS 항목이 함께 제시됨

## 용도
- Tapping STI Threads of Soft Materials
- Soft materials screw thread insert tapping

## 선정 가이드
- For STI thread tapping in soft materials, choose HSS-E spiral flute for max. 2.5xD blind holes and HSS straight flute for max. 2.0xD blind/through holes. The page maps the STI series to M/MF, UNC/F, W, M-LH/W-LH, and pipe tap variants, with Bright surface treatment.
