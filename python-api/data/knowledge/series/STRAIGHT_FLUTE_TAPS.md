# YG-1 — STRAIGHT FLUTE TAPS
**제품명**: STRAIGHT FLUTE TAPS for CAST IRONS / 铸铁用直槽丝锥

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Straight Flute
- 생크: Male Center (up to W5/16)
- 직경 범위: W1/8~W1"
- 깊이비: 2.0×D

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Grey Cast Iron
- Nodular Cast Iron
- Malleable Cast Iron
- Brass Casting
- similar materials

## 특징
- Suitable for tapping Blind & Through holes
- Hole type: 2.0×D
- 1.5P / 5.0P
- up to W5/16 : Male Center

## 용도
- Tapping blind and through holes in cast irons and similar materials

## 선정 가이드
- Recommended for cast iron applications; application table shows excellent/good suitability mainly for grey cast iron, nodular cast iron, and malleable cast iron. Refer to p.B233-B238 for recommended tap drill sizes.
