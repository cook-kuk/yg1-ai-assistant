# YG-1 — E2170
**제품명**: 8% COBALT (M42) FLAT SHANK E2170 SERIES / HSS Co8, MULTI FLUTE REGULAR LENGTH COARSE PITCH ROUGHING

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Roughing / Flat Shank
- 날 수: 3, 4, 5, 6, 8
- 공구 재질: HSS Co8 (M42)
- 코팅: TiN / TiCN / TiAlN F / TiAlN E / Hardslick (available on request)
- 생크: Flat Shank / Combination Shank
- 직경 범위: 1/4" to 2"

## 특징
- General purpose rougher designed for high production metal removal
- Recommended for cutting steel grades and many non-ferrous materials
- End tooth has a center hole design for many accurate resharpenings between centers
- Multi flute, regular length, coarse pitch
- Combination shank available on some sizes

## 용도
- High production metal removal
- Roughing in a wide range of workpiece materials
- Cutting steel grades
- Cutting many non-ferrous materials

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
