# OSG Solid Carbide Lineup

Sources: https://www.osgtool.com/exocarb-aero-dlc-2974, https://www.osgtool.com/pxm, https://osgtool.com/phoenix-pxsh

## PHOENIX PXM (Exchangeable head end mill)
- Modular carbide head end mill
- Square + corner radius heads; multi-flute
- DLC-IGUSS coating for non-ferrous (PXAL variant)
- XP4625 grade
- For ISO N (aluminum/non-ferrous) and general steel

## PHOENIX PXSH (Multi-flute square)
- Multi-flute square & corner radius
- Inch + metric

## EXOCARB AERO DLC
- 3-flute carbide end mill for aluminum alloys
- DLC coating, square + corner radius, solid + coolant-through
- ISO N (aluminum)

## EXOCARB WXS / WXL (Hardened steels)
- High-hardness material end mills, up to 70 HRC
- Premium WXS coating

Operations: slotting, roughing, finishing, profiling
