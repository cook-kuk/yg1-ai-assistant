# CVD Diamond — National Diamond Tool, American Mold Builder, SAMHO
- Hardness ~9000 HV (88–98 GPa)
- Thickness 8–20 µm (some 3–5 µm micro-grain)
- Friction 0.05–0.30
- Excellent thermal conductivity; CANNOT be used for ferrous (graphitization)
- Ideal: graphite, CFRP, GFRP, high-Si Al, ceramics, green carbide
Source: https://americanmoldbuilder.com/articles/2017/basics-of-cvd-diamond-coated-cutting-tools/
