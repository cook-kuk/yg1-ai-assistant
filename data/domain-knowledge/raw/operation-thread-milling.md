# Thread milling — Kennametal TMS

Source: https://www.kennametal.com/us/en/products/metalworking-tools/threading/thread-milling.html
Source: https://www.kennametal.com/us/en/products/p.tms-thread-milling-system-thread-mills-normal-inch.1280481.html

- Thread mill produces internal/external threads via helical interpolation.
- Works in any material; one tool covers multiple pitches (single-profile) or a pitch family (multi-profile).
- Kennametal recommends using NOVO / engineering calculators for Vc/fz per material and insert.
- Advantages over tapping: controlled chip, no breakage in blind holes, superior in hard/tough materials.
- Radial ramping into full depth (arc in/out) avoids witness marks.
