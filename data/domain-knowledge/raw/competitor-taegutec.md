# TaeguTec Solid Carbide + Indexable

Sources: https://www.taegutec.com/pages/wp-content/uploads/kboard_attached/11/202405/664c314958e2c5203815.pdf, https://www.imc-companies.com/TaeguTec/ttkCatalog/

## HARD-MILL
- Solid carbide end mill for high-hardness material machining (up to 65 HRC)
- Flat, radius and ball nose types
- Wide selection of neck lengths
- ISO H

## TOP-MILL (TopMill)
- General-purpose solid carbide end mill, 4-flute
- ISO P / M / K

## ChaseMill (APKT 09 / 17 / 19 indexable)
- Indexable shoulder/face mill
- APKT inserts, various sizes
- High productivity roughing

## ChaseFeed / MillRush (High-feed indexable)
- High-feed face milling
- Trigon inserts
