# Tapping — Sandvik Coromant / Kennametal

Source: https://www.sandvik.coromant.com/en-us/knowledge/threading/tapping

- Cutting tap (spiral-flute for blind, spiral-point for through) vs. forming tap (no chips, stronger thread).
- Vc typically 5–15 m/min for cutting taps in steel; higher for forming taps.
- Synchronized spindle tapping or tension/compression holder required.
- Cutting fluid / oil mandatory — tapping is high-friction.
- Pre-drill diameter per ISO; forming tap pre-drill is larger than cutting tap.
