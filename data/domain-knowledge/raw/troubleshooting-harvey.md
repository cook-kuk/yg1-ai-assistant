## Chip packing (deep pocket)
- Tool breakage along LOC → chip packing likely.
- Decrease feed and ap per pass; increase coolant/air pressure.
- Use fewer-flute tools with deeper valleys.

## Built-up edge (BUE)
- TiB2 coating has extremely low affinity to aluminum → prevents BUE and chip packing.
- Increase Vc above critical BUE speed.

## Deep pocket milling
- Challenges: tool deflection, chip evacuation, tool reach.
- Excess overhang is leading cause of deflection — use necked-down reach tools.

## Thin-wall deflection
- High-feed end mills good for low-HP machines and thin walls.
- Maximize shank contact in collet; minimize stickout.
- Use largest possible end mill diameter → less deflection.
- Climb mill and alternate wall stepdown (waterfall strategy).
