# Drilling — Sandvik Coromant / Harvey

Source: https://www.sandvik.coromant.com/en-us/knowledge/machining-formulas-definitions/pages/drilling.aspx

- Point angle: 118° general, 130–140° stainless/super alloys, 90° brass/cast iron.
- L/D > 3: internal-coolant through-tool drill strongly recommended.
- Peck drilling for L/D > 5 or gummy materials (stainless, aluminum alloys).
- Pre-spot with a center drill/spotting drill to avoid walking.
- Feed (fn) scales with diameter; Vc per material grade in Sandvik CoroKey.
