# Dynamic / Trochoidal Milling — Kennametal (excerpt)
Source: https://www.kennametal.com/us/en/resources/blog/dynamic-milling.html

- Dynamic (trochoidal) milling uses small radial (ae 5–10% D) with full axial engagement (ap up to 2×D).
- The full flute length shares the heat — each tooth engages briefly, cools during rotation.
- Typical MRR parity or better than conventional slotting while using 1.5–2× the fz.
- Ideal for ISO M/S (stainless, titanium, Inconel), hardened steels, and deep slots.
- Requires CAM with constant-engagement toolpath (Mastercam Dynamic, hyperMILL MAXX, Fusion Adaptive, etc.).
- Benefits: 3–10× tool life, reduced chatter, consistent chip load, axial force direction.
