# Korloy Solid + Indexable

Sources: https://www.korloy.com/en/prdinfo/view.do?id_product=134, https://www.korloyeurope.eu/media/Downloads-Fraesen-EN/KORLOY%20Solid%20Endmill%20Brochure%202021.pdf

## A+ Endmill
- Solid carbide end mill
- 4-flute square, corner radius, ball nose
- Diameter 1-20 mm
- AlCrN-based coating
- ISO P / M / K general

## D Endmill
- Solid carbide end mill for hardened steel
- Up to 60+ HRC

## HRM (High-feed indexable)
- HRMDSA high-feed indexable end mill
- Negative trigon 6-edge inserts
- 1.5" and larger diameters
- High-feed roughing

## AMS (Indexable 90 deg shoulder mill)
- AMSA1050HS-2, APMT0602 inserts
- 0.5" diameter, Weldon

## AMP (Indexable)
- Pocket mill
