# TiSiN — ScienceDirect, Kyocera HDT
- Nanocomposite (nc-TiN / a-Si3N4)
- Max hardness ~3700 HV at 6–8 at.% Si (≈40 GPa)
- Oxidation resistance 700–1100°C depending on Si content
- Used as top layer on AlTiN for very hard & abrasive work (hardened steels >55 HRC)
Source: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0257897217304036
