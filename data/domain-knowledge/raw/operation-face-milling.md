# Face milling — Sandvik Coromant

Source: https://www.sandvik.coromant.com/en-us/knowledge/milling/face-milling

- Face milling cutters selected by ap and fz; general-purpose inserts cover most needs.
- Max recommended chip thickness up to ~0.55 mm/tooth depending on insert geometry.
- Finishing: PVD-coated sharp edges, ap 0.5–0.8 mm for best Ra.
- Heavy-duty: combine high ap + high fz + large ae for productivity.
- Thin walls: low Vc, small ap, low fz to avoid deflection.
- Programmed entry/exit angles reduce insert chipping on entry.
