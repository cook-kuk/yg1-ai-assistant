# Emuge-Franken + YMW Taps & Thread Mills

Sources: https://www.emuge-franken-group.com/us/en/threading-technology/, https://www.emuge.com/thread-mills/carbide-thread-mills

## Emuge VHM-Rekord 1A
- Solid carbide machine tap (Rekord 1A-HCUT/C-TICN)
- TiCN coating
- High-performance cutting taps for steel / stainless
- Metric + inch
- ISO P / M

## Emuge THREADS-ALL (GFI / GFR thread mills)
- Solid carbide thread mill, single-profile
- Internal + external thread milling
- 3-5 flutes
- TiCN / TiAlN coatings
- Universal ISO P / M / K / S

## Emuge BGF-Z4 (solid carbide thread mill)
- 4-flute 1.5xD R20 coolant-through
- TiAlN coating
- Internal threading

## YMW USA (part of Emuge-Franken)
- YMW Zelx high-performance taps
- ISO P / M / K
- Similar positioning to YG-1 Combo Tap
