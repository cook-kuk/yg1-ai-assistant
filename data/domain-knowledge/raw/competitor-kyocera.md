# Kyocera / Kyocera Unimerco

Sources: https://asia.kyocera.com/products/cuttingtools/product/milling/mfh-series/, https://www.kyocera-unimerco.com/en-us/tools/metal/milling/end-mills/solid-end-mills/q-series/

## MFH-Raptor / MFH-Max / MFH-Mini / MFH-Micro (Indexable High-Feed)
- Indexable high-feed face/shoulder mill
- 4 chipbreakers, 5 insert grades
- Large-diameter (MFH-Raptor) to micro (MFH-Micro)
- ISO P / M / K high-feed roughing

## MFK (Indexable Face Mill)
- Face milling cutter, multi-edge inserts

## Q-Series (Kyocera Unimerco Solid Carbide End Mills)
- Q-Standard: general-purpose 4-flute
- Variable helix, AlTiN PVD
- Diameter 1-20 mm
- ISO P / M / K

Operations: high-feed roughing, finishing, face milling
