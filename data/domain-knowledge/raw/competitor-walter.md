# Walter Prototyp Solid Carbide

Sources: https://www.walter-tools.com/en-us/tools/standard_products/milling/overview/shoulder_milling/protostar_n_50/pages/default.aspx

## Proto-max (H4033217 / H4034217 / H4134217)
- 3-flute and 4-flute carbide end mills
- Diameters 3-20 mm; shank cylindrical
- TAZ / TiAlN (AH) coating, stainless variant "Inox"
- ConeFit exchangeable carbide head variants (H2EC34217)
- Universal ISO P/M/K

## Protostar N50
- Shoulder milling solid carbide
- For steel and cast iron

## MC232 / MC326 / MC025 (Walter Prototyp SC Endmill Line)
- MC232: general 4-flute for steel
- MC326: ISO M stainless steel optimized
- MC025: finishing

Operations: shoulder, slot, face, ramping
