# CRX — SGED270
**제품명**: CRX S END MILLS / CARBIDE, 2 FLUTE BALL NOSE DLC COATING WITH EXTENDED NECK

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Ball Nose / Extended Neck
- 날 수: 2
- 공구 재질: CARBIDE
- 코팅: DLC
- 생크: Straight shank
- 직경 범위: D1.5~D12.0

## 대응 ISO 그룹
N

## 특징
- 2 flute ball nose end mill with extended neck
- DLC coated
- tight radius tolerance (±0.005mm under R3)
- excellent surface roughness thanks to Mirror Face of cutting edges
- high strength and minimized vibration due to two step taper neck (under R0.5)
