# YG-1 — D4107
**제품명**: HSS Co8, STRAIGHT SHANK DRILL - STUB

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Drill
- 서브타입: Straight Shank / Stub
- 공구 재질: HSS Co8
- 코팅: TiN
- 헬릭스각: 33°
- 선단각: 135°
- 생크: Straight shank
- 직경 범위: D5.8~D11.5

## 특징
- Right hand spiral helix
- Point angle 135°
- Under 1.6mm: normal point
- 1.6mm & over: split point
- Suitable for drilling in thin materials with portable drills
- Special twist drills for automatic and turret lathes
- HSS Co5 (DL107) is available when needed
- TiN, TiCN and TiAlN coatings are available on request
- 5pcs per package

## 용도
- Drilling in thin materials with portable drills
- Automatic lathes
- Turret lathes
