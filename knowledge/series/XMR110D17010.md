## 실적 / 케이스 스터디
- Diamond coated i-Xmill Corner Radius (XMR110D17010) was tested on graphite with cutting speed Vc 320 m/min, RPM 6,000, feed 2,800 mm/min, feed per tooth 0.23 mm/tooth, axial depth 0.2 mm, and air cooling. The page states that the diamond coating improves tool life, enables higher cutting speeds, and delivers better quality in graphite machining.
- TEST III shows i-Xmill Corner Radius (XMR110D17010) cutting graphite with Diamond Coating at Vc 320 m/min, 6,000 RPM, feed 2,800 mm/min, feed per tooth 0.23 mm/tooth, axial depth 0.2 mm, and air coolant. The graph indicates tool life of about 52 hours for Diamond Coating versus about 6 hours for Conventional Coating.
- Diamond coated i-Xmill corner radius tool test on graphite showed improved tool life. The test used size Ø17 x R1.0, SFM 1052 ft/min, RPM 108, feed per tooth .009 mm/tooth, axial milling depth .008 inch, and air coolant.
