# YG-1 — UGMF73
**제품명**: CARBIDE, 4 FLUTE EXTENDED REACH

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Extended Reach / Square
- 날 수: 4
- 공구 재질: CARBIDE
- 코팅: Advanced coating
- 헬릭스각: multiple helix; 37° single-helix mentioned
- 생크: h5 (≥ Ø1/2": h6)
- 직경 범위: 3/8"~1"
- 깊이비: Extended reach; LOC up to 1-1/8" with OAL up to 6"

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Non-alloy steel
- Low alloy steel
- High alloyed steel
- Tool steel
- Stainless steel
- Grey cast iron
- Nodular cast iron
- Malleable cast iron
- Aluminum-wrought alloy
- Aluminum-cast alloyed
- Copper and Copper Alloys
- Non Metallic Materials
- Heat Resistant Super Alloys
- Titanium Alloys
- Hardened steel
- Chilled Cast Iron
- Hardened Cast Iron

## 특징
- Special flute geometry and multiple helix eliminate vibrations
- Reinforced cutting edge
- Excellent performance for stainless steels, mild steels, cast iron, low/medium hardness materials and all exotic materials up to HRc40
- Advanced coating for superior performance and tool life
- Length of cut in excess of 3xD on 37° single-helix requires feed reduction of approximately 50%

## 용도
- General milling in stainless steels, mild steels, cast iron, low/medium hardness materials
- Machining exotic materials up to HRc40

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
- [page_1513] CARBIDE, 4 FLUTE EXTENDED REACH 시리즈의 치수표 페이지이다. Square와 Corner Radius 두 형식이 있으며, 특수 flute geometry와 multiple helix로 진동을 줄이고 reinforced cutting edge 및 advanced coating을 적용했다. 스테인리스, 연강, 주철, 저/중경도 재료와 HRc40 이하의 exotic materials에 우수하며, 3xD를 초과하는 37° single-helix 조건에서는 이송을 약 50% 줄여야 한다.
