- 공구 타입: Tap

- HSS tap series
- TiN coating option
- Max. 3.0xD Through Hole
- General purpose spiral point tap
- Plug style
- Thread depth / hole type: 3.0×D
- Available in Bright, Steam Oxide, and TiN finishes
