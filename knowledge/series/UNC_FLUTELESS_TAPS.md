# YG-1 — UNC FLUTELESS TAPS
**제품명**: UNC FLUTELESS TAPS for NON-FERROUS METALS / 有色金属用挤压丝锥

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Fluteless / Forming Tap
- 공구 재질: HSS-E
- 코팅: Bright
- 선단각: 60°
- 생크: Plain Shank
- 직경 범위: #2-56 UNC to 1/4-20 UNC
- 깊이비: 3.0×D

## 대응 ISO 그룹
N

## 대상 피삭재
- Aluminum-wrought alloy
- Aluminum-cast alloyed
- Copper and Copper Alloys (Bronze / Brass)

## 특징
- Suitable for tapping in Aluminum, Magnesium, Zinc, Copper as well as Non-Ferrous metal alloys.
- 适用于铝, 镁, 铜及有色金属的加工.
- Hole type: 3.0×D
- Recommended ToolHolder: Plain Shank, TAPPING ER CHUCK (D215-220), TAPPING CHUCK (D221-228), ONE STEP TAPPING CHUCK (D211-213)

## 용도
- Tapping in non-ferrous metals
- Aluminum, Magnesium, Zinc, Copper and non-ferrous metal alloys
