- 서브타입: Spiral Point Tap / Plug Style
- 날 수: 2 or 3
- 공구 재질: HSS PM
- 코팅: Steam Oxide / Bright / TiCN / Hardslick
- 생크: ANSI
- 직경 범위: No.2 ~ 1/2
- 깊이비: 3.0×D

- Nickel Base Alloys

- Designed for titanium alloys and nickel base alloys up to HRC38~HRC45
- Available in multiple surface finishes/coatings: Steam Oxide, Bright, TiCN, Hardslick
- Plug style spiral point tap

- Threading nickel base alloys
