## 선정 가이드
- M, UNC, UNF forms are suitable for Max. 2.0xD blind/through holes; MF form is suitable for Max. 1.5xD blind/through holes. Material suitability is rated ◎ for P/K/N and ○ for M/S, with H group not rated on this page.
