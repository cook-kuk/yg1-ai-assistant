# YG TAP HARDENED — T0999-TIC
**제품명**: T0999-TIC

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Hardened
- 공구 재질: Carbide
- 코팅: TICN
- 생크: DIN 371 / DIN 376
- 직경 범위: M3~M20
- 깊이비: 2.0xD

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, N, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Non-alloy steel
- Stainless steel
- Copper and Copper Alloys (Bronze / Brass)
- Hardened steel
- Chilled Cast Iron
- Hardened Cast Iron
- Hardened steels (HRC50~60)

## 특징
- Carbide tap can increase tool life longer than HSS taps due to higher hardness
- Suitable for hardened steels (HRC50~60)
- Hole type: 2.0xD
- DIN 371 (M3~M10) and DIN 376 (M12~M20)

## 용도
- ISO metric coarse threads DIN 13 tapping
