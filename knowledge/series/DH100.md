## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
- [page_0155] HSS-E straight shank twist drills for deep holes are shown in LONG and JOBBER versions under the DH100 worm pattern drills series. The LONG version follows DIN 340 with 42° helix, h8 tolerance, 130° point angle and Bright finish; the JOBBER version follows DIN 338 with 38° helix, h8 tolerance, 130° point angle and Vap/steam tempered black oxide finish. Applications include deep-hole drilling in non-alloy and alloy steels, cast irons, and special aluminum or magnesium alloys, with optional TiN, TiCN and TiAlN coatings available on request.
