## 선정 가이드
- Other diameters and shank types are available upon request.
- For inclined surfaces, adjust feed rate by surface angle: 0°~15° use 100% RPM and 100% feed; 15°~30° use 100% RPM and 50% feed; 30° or more use 70% RPM and 30% feed. Recommended hole depth is measured from the highest point on inclined/angled surfaces. Decrease cutting speed as material hardness increases.
