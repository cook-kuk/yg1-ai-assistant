## 선정 가이드
- Selection guide for YG TAP INOX. For UNC/UNF, H0 is indicated with Hardslick coating and HSS-PM tool material. ◎ means Excellent and ○ means Good; the material suitability table rates each ISO material group by VDI 3323 hardness ranges.
- For stainless steels, use HSS-PM tool material with spiral flute (R45) and 2P-3P chamfer lead for max. 2.5xD blind holes. The guide shows G7, G8, G9, and H0 as applicable models for UNC/UNF, with coatings split between TiN and Hardslick.
