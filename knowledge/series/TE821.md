# YG-1 — TE821
**제품명**: TE821 SERIES ISO metric coarse threads DIN 13 / Machine taps

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Machine tap
- 날 수: 3-4
- 공구 재질: HSS-E
- 코팅: Nitride
- 선단각: 60°
- 생크: Plain Shank
- 직경 범위: M2~M30
- 깊이비: 2.0×D

## 대응 ISO 그룹
K

## 대상 피삭재
- GG
- Grey cast iron
- Nodular cast iron
- Malleable cast iron

## 특징
- ISO metric coarse threads DIN 13
- Suitable for tapping cast iron or similar work materials
- DIN 371 / DIN 376
- Hole type: 2.0×D
- 6HX tolerance
- p.B205
- Tolerance 6HX
- Hole Type 2.0×D
- DIN 371(M2~M10) and DIN 376(M11~M30)
- DIN profile not ISO

## 용도
- Tapping cast iron or similar work materials

## 선정 가이드
- Recommended for ISO K materials; applicability table shows ◎ for grey cast iron, nodular cast iron, and malleable cast iron, and ○ for copper/brass. DIN 371 applies to M2~M10 and DIN 376 to M11~M30; DIN profile not ISO for starred sizes.
