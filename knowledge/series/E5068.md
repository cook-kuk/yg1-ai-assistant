# YG-1 — E5068
**제품명**: PLAIN SHANK E5068 SERIES / CARBIDE, 5 FLUTE 45° HELIX MEDIUM & LONG LENGTH

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Plain Shank
- 날 수: 5
- 공구 재질: CARBIDE
- 코팅: Uncoated, TiN, TiCN, YG:TYLON F, YG:TYLON E
- 헬릭스각: 45°
- 생크: Plain Shank
- 직경 범위: 1/4~1

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Stainless steel
- Inconels
- Other alloys

## 특징
- Designed to machine stainless steel, inconels and other alloys
- New stub length design allows cutting at maximum speeds and feeds with minimum deflection
- 5 flute and 45° medium helix allow harmonic balance and smooth cutting

## 용도
- Machining stainless steel
- Machining inconels
- Machining other alloys
