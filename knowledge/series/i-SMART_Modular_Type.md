## 실적 / 케이스 스터디
- YG-1 i-SMART Modular Type 4-flute corner radius end mill (Ø16 x R1.0) was tested in down & side cutting over a total milling length of 60m. Cutting conditions were Vc 155.82 m/min, 3,100 rpm, feed 280 mm/min, fz 0.02 mm/tooth, axial depth 12 mm, radial depth 0.8 mm, overhang 77 mm, with wet cutting on a machining center.
