# 4G MILL — SEME710
**제품명**: CARBIDE, 4 FLUTE MULTIPLE HELIX (Sharp Corner Removal)

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Multiple Helix / Sharp Corner Removal
- 날 수: 4
- 공구 재질: CARBIDE
- 헬릭스각: Multiple Helix; single helix 38° for long length
- 생크: Short / Regular / Long shank; 4mm shank available for D2.5
- 직경 범위: D2.5~D6.0

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Prehardened steel
- Carbon steel
- Alloy steel
- Mold and die steel
- Steel up to HRC55
- Machine parts

## 특징
- New coating and tool geometry applied resulting outstanding cutting abilities and wear resistance
- Excellent for cutting prehardened steels, carbon steels, alloy steels of molds and dies, up to HRC55 and machine parts
- Multiple Helix for 3.0mm and over 3.0mm diameter endmills minimizing vibration and decreasing wear in cutting
- Equal index flutes design for long length and single helix (38°) end mills
- Gash land geometry applied at the end tooth, achieving heavy duty cutting
- Available various length products like short, regular and long length end mills
- Available in short, regular and long shank end mills

## 용도
- Cutting prehardened steels
- Cutting carbon steels
- Cutting alloy steels of molds and dies
- Machine parts machining

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
- [page_1287] 4G MILL CARBIDE 4날 멀티 헬릭스(Sharp Corner Removal) 엔드밀 시리즈이다. 신코팅과 신규 공구 형상으로 절삭성능과 내마모성이 우수하며, 3.0mm 이상에서는 멀티 헬릭스로 진동과 마모를 줄인다. 프리하든강, 탄소강, 합금강, 금형강 및 HRC55까지의 가공과 기계부품에 적합하고, 숏/레귤러/롱 및 롱 샹크 형식이 있다.
