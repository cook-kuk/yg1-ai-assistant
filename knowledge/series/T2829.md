- N: Aluminum cast alloy
- N: Copper and copper alloys
- S: Heat resistant super alloys

## 특징
- For multi purpose tapping
- Max. 3.0xD through hole
- Spiral point type
- Chamber lead acc. to DIN2197: 5.0P
- JIS Type I

## 용도
- Through hole tapping

## 선정 가이드
- Use T2829 for max. 3.0xD through holes. It is a spiral point combo tap with HSS-E material, 5.0P chamfer lead, JIS Type I, and bright surface treatment.
