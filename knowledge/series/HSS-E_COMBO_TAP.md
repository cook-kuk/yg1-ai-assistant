# YG-1 — HSS-E COMBO TAP
**제품명**: HSS-E COMBO TAP

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Tap
- 서브타입: Combo Tap / Spiral Flute
- 공구 재질: HSS-E
- 코팅: Steam Oxide / TiCN
- 헬릭스각: R40
- 생크: DIN Length-ANSI Shank / USCTI Long Shank

## 대응 ISO 그룹
P, M, K, N, S, H

## 대상 피삭재
- Non-alloy steel
- Low alloy steel
- High alloyed steel and tool steel
- Stainless steel
- Grey cast iron
- Nodular cast iron
- Malleable cast iron
- Aluminum-wrought alloy
- Aluminum-cast alloy
- Copper and copper alloys
- Non metallic materials
- Heat resistant super alloys
- Titanium alloys
- Hardened steel
- Chilled cast iron
- Hardened cast iron

## 특징
- HSS-E taps for multi-purpose
- Chamfer lead 2P-3P
- Spiral flute type
- Available with Steam Oxide or TiCN surface treatment/coating
- Referenced tap standards include USCTI 302A, USCTI 302, DIN Length-ANSI Shank

## 용도
- General-purpose tapping across a wide range of materials
- Selection guide for hole type and material compatibility

## 선정 가이드
- HSS-E Combo Tap is recommended as a multi-purpose tap. The guide marks material suitability with ◎ (Excellent) and 〇 (Good), with strongest applicability shown for steels, stainless steels, cast irons, copper alloys, and some hardened materials depending on the row. For some materials such as aluminum, non-metallics, and certain superalloys/titanium, no rating is shown.

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
- [page_0625] HSS-E COMBO TAP은 다목적용 HSS-E 탭으로, 2P-3P 챔퍼 리드와 Spiral Flute, R40 나선각을 사용한다. 표면처리는 Steam Oxide와 TiCN이 있으며, USCTI 302A/302, DIN Length-ANSI Shank, USCTI Long Shank 등의 규격이 제시된다. 선택 가이드는 P/M/K/N/S/H 전반의 다양한 재질에 대한 적합성을 ◎/〇로 표시하며, 강, 스테인리스, 주철, 구리합금 등에 폭넓게 적용 가능함을 보여준다.
