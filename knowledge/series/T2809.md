## 선정 가이드
- Use T2809 for max. 2.5xD blind holes. It is a spiral flute combo tap with HSS-E material, 2.5P chamfer lead, R40 spiral flute angle, JIS Type I, and bright surface treatment.
- Refer to p.B233-B238 for recommended tap drill sizes. Plain shank compatible with TAPPING ER CHUCK, TAPPING CHUCK, and ONE STEP TAPPING CHUCK.
