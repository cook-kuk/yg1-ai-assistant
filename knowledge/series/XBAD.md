# i-Xmill — XBAD
**제품명**: i-Xmill BALL INSERTS / XBAD SERIES

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: End Mill
- 서브타입: Ball Insert / Indexable Ball End Mill
- 코팅: extremely abrasive resistant coating
- 직경 범위: D8.0~D32.0

## 대응 ISO 그룹
N, S

## 대상 피삭재
- Aluminum-wrought alloy
- Aluminum-cast alloyed
- Copper and Copper Alloys
- Non Metallic Materials
- Aluminum-cast, alloyed
- Graphite

## 특징
- Indexable Ball End Mill for economic use
- Three types of inserts are available
- For Graphite
- Special geometry
- Excellent performance
- Ball inserts for graphite
- Recommended cutting conditions provided by ISO material group and tool diameter

## 용도
- Graphite machining
- Machining graphite

## 선정 가이드
- ◎ : Excellent, ○ : Good. XBAD is the graphite version; the matrix shows excellent suitability for non-metallic and some N/S materials, while the hardened/steel groups are not selected.

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
