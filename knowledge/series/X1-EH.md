## 실적 / 케이스 스터디
- Case study page showing X1-EH end mills tested on hardened SKD11/D2 material. Test I used an Ø3(R1.5) 2-flute ball nose for profiling with 21,000 rpm and 2,800 mm/min feed, achieving 248 m total milling length. Test II used an Ø6 2-flute square end mill for side cutting with 4,200 rpm and 255 mm/min feed, achieving 2 m total milling length.
- TEST III profiling and TEST IV face milling on STAVAX(HRc52)/AISI 420 showed X1-EH wear resistance better than competitor, with wear reduced from 44µm to 22µm in profiling and from 62µm to 23µm in face milling.
- Page shows cutting test results for X1-EH end mills. Test I used an Ø3(R1.5) 2 flute ball nose for profiling on SKD11/D2 at 21,000 rpm and 110 in/min, achieving a total milling length of 813.6 ft. Test II used an Ø6 2 flute square tool for side cutting on the same material at 4,200 rpm and 10 in/min, achieving a total milling length of 6.6 ft.
- TEST III profiling application and TEST IV face milling application show YG-1 X1-EH outperforming a competitor in wear resistance. In profiling, competitor wear was 44 µm versus 22 µm for YG-1 X1-EH over 2,034 ft of total milling length. In face milling, competitor wear was 62 µm versus 23 µm for YG-1 X1-EH over 262.5 ft.
