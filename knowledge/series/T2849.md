## 선정 가이드
- Use T2849 for max. 3.0xD through holes. It is the UNC/F version of the spiral point combo tap with HSS-E material, 5.0P chamfer lead, JIS Type I, and bright surface treatment.
- Material groups: MU (HSS-E, I, YH, 60°, 5.0P, Bright). Tap Limits: p.B230. Tool holder recommendations: Plain Shank D215-220, TAPPING ER CHUCK D215-220, TAPPING CHUCK D221-228, ONE STEP TAPPING CHUCK D211-213. Refer to p.B233-B238 for recommended tap drill sizes.

## 상세 정보 (원문 요약)
