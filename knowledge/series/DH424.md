- Recommended for ISO P/M/K materials; also shown as suitable for some N/S/H categories in the application table. Recommended toolholders: Plain Shank D47-72, Shrink Fit Holder D47-72, Hydraulic Chuck D15-46, ER Collet Chuck D73-115.
