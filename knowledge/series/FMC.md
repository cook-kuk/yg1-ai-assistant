# YG-1 — FMC
**제품명**: FACE MILL ARBOR

## 기본 사양
- 공구 타입: Arbor
- 서브타입: Face Mill
- 생크: BT (JIS B6339/MAS 403) / CBT (BT DUAL CONTACT) / HSK (DIN 69893/ISO 12164)

## 특징
- Standard type
- Available with BT, CBT dual contact, and HSK shanks
- HSK Form A face mill arbor
- Dimensioned with D, C, L, H, K, G
- Available in taper 63A

## 용도
- Face milling
- Face milling tool holding
